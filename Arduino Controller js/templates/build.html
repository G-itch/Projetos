Build steps

What you will need?

1x Arduino or ESP
1x IR sensor
1x Controller
1x Protoboard
3x Wires

How to build:

-Attach the IR sensor in the protoboard

- Connect the wires in the sequence:
1: PIN 4
2: GND
3: VCC

-Plug the Arduino in your desktop

Visualization:

 You can solder the wires directly in the sensor if you want, the two options are valid.
